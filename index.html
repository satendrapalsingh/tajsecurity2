About Us

Taj Security Agency is an ISO certified agency (ISO Certificate No. 18DQCK35) registered in Rajasthan & UP under the Private Security Agencies (Regulation) Act of 2005. We have also worked extensively under the sponsorship of Govt. of India, MOD, Director General of Resettlement, R K Puram, New Delhi (Reg. No. 2010).
