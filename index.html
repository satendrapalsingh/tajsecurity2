About Us

Taj Security Agency is an ISO certified agency registered in Rajasthan & UP under the Private Security Agencies (Regulation) Act of 2005. We have also worked extensively under the sponsorship of Govt. of India, MOD, Director General of Resettlement, R K Puram, New Delhi (Reg. No. 2010).

Our Services

We provide security and house-keeping services including supporting services such as Electronic Surveillance, Traffic & Parking Management, Fire Protection Management Systems etc.
